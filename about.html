About PriceGap

PriceGap explains real price gaps, hidden costs, and buyer tradeoffs across hosting, VPNs, password managers, and software subscriptions.

What PriceGap covers

PriceGap is a buyer-education site focused on price transparency. We help readers understand the difference between advertised prices and real long-term cost before they choose a service or product.

Our current coverage includes web hosting, VPS decisions, VPN plans, password managers, and software subscriptions where renewal pricing, add-ons, support, refund windows, and switching costs change the real cost.

Our editorial approach

  • We compare pricing structures, not just headline discounts.
  • We highlight renewals, add-ons, refund windows, support limits, privacy/security tradeoffs, and migration or switching costs.
  • We avoid claiming current prices when they may change; readers should verify current terms directly before buying.
  • Older product-import content has been removed; the site now focuses on services and software price transparency.

Contact

Contact details may be added as the site matures. For now, PriceGap is maintained as an independent static content site.
