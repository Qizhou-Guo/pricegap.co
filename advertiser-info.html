Advertiser Information

Information for advertiser and affiliate-network reviewers about PriceGap audiences, content categories, and compliance principles.

Site summary

PriceGap is a price-transparency and buyer-education site. It helps readers compare advertised offers against real long-term cost across hosting, VPS, VPN, password manager, and software subscription decisions.

Audience

  • Small business owners and site builders comparing hosting plans.
  • Privacy-conscious consumers evaluating VPN pricing and tradeoffs.
  • Families, freelancers, and teams deciding whether a password manager plan is worth the cost.

Current content categories

  • Hosting & VPS: renewal pricing, shared hosting vs VPS, 3-year cost comparisons.
  • VPN & Privacy: monthly vs multi-year plans, free vs paid VPN risks.
  • Password Managers: family vs individual plans, browser passwords vs paid tools.

Compliance principles

  • We do not claim approval by any advertiser unless an approval exists.
  • We do not use cloaking, forced redirects, hidden links, or misleading download/checkout buttons.
  • We do not present ourselves as an official brand site.
  • We do not make brand-bidding claims or imply permission to bid on brand terms.
  • We avoid stale price claims and encourage readers to verify current prices and renewal terms.
  • We separate educational content from affiliate disclosure and policy pages.

Review note

PriceGap is designed to send informed, comparison-oriented readers to relevant advertisers only when content fit and program rules allow it.
