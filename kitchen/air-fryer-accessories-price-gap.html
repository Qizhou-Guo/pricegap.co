Why Air Fryer Accessories Have Such a Big Markup

Air fryers became one of the fastest-selling kitchen appliances of the past decade. By 2025, an estimated 40% of US households own one. With that adoption comes a predictable wave of demand: replacement parts, add-on accessories, and upgrade trays.

The problem is that most people replace their accessories through the same channel they bought the appliance — Amazon, Walmart, or the manufacturer's own store — without realizing that the exact same parts are manufactured in the same Chinese factories and sold directly on AliExpress for a fraction of the price.

The markup isn't driven by quality differences. It's driven by brand packaging, retail distribution margins, and consumer search behavior. Most buyers don't know to search AliExpress for "replacement air fryer basket compatible with Ninja" — so they pay the Amazon premium instead.

What Accessories Are Actually in Demand

The air fryer accessories market breaks down into a few clear categories:

  • Replacement baskets: The most commonly replaced part. Original air fryer baskets wear out, lose non-stick coating, or crack.
  • Crisper trays and racks: Perforated metal trays that go inside the basket for better airflow and crispiness.
  • Accessory sets: Bundles of divider, pizza stone, metal rack, silicone pot, and recipe book — sold as upgrade kits.
  • Silicone liners: Non-stick mats that protect the basket and reduce cleanup. (We covered these in detail in our dedicated article.)
  • Toasting racks and baking pans: Flat metal inserts for small pizzas, cookies, and frozen snacks.

Why the Same Part Costs So Much Less on AliExpress

The price gap between AliExpress and Amazon for air fryer accessories comes from several overlapping factors:

1. Middlemen and Distribution Margins

On Amazon, every product goes through a chain: manufacturer → exporter → Amazon seller → Amazon FBA (or sellerfulfilled) → buyer. Each step adds a margin. A product that costs $5 to produce and ship from China carries a $5–$8 AliExpress retail price with a $1–$2 seller's margin. The same product on Amazon goes through an exporter who buys at $6, a seller who lists at $18, and Amazon who takes a referral fee — bringing the final retail price to $20–$27.

2. Amazon's Fee Structure

Amazon charges sellers 15–25% in referral fees plus FBA storage and fulfillment costs. For a $20 product, Amazon fees alone can be $4–$6. AliExpress sellers operate with lower platform fees and ship directly from China, which means they can price aggressively and still profit at $7–$10.

3. Brand Awareness vs. Compatibility

Amazon buyers often search by brand name: "Ninja air fryer replacement basket." This drives them to official-looking listings with Ninja branding that charge a premium. On AliExpress, the same buyers search "Ninja AF100UK basket replacement" and find compatible parts priced at a fraction of the cost. The parts are often identical — or from the same supplier — but the search behavior keeps Amazon prices high.

4. Shipping and Wait Time

AliExpress accessories typically ship from China with a 10–25 day delivery window. Amazon offers Prime 1–2 day delivery. That convenience premium is worth paying for some buyers — but for a replacement basket you don't need immediately, the 3× savings on AliExpress is hard to argue against.

What to Watch Out For

Size and compatibility: Air fryer basket dimensions vary by model and even within the same brand across generations. Before buying, measure your existing basket or check the model number in the product listing. Most AliExpress sellers list compatible models — but always verify before ordering.

Beyond size, there are a few other things worth checking:

  • Non-stick coating type: Look for PFOA-free / PTFE-free ceramic or silicone-based coatings. Cheap listings may not specify, which raises quality questions.
  • Material grade: Food-grade stainless steel (304 grade) is standard for crisper trays. Verify the listing specifies this if it's important to you.
  • Seller ratings and reviews: AliExpress seller ratings above 95% and detailed review photos help confirm quality. Look for listings with 100+ reviews and photo reviews showing the actual product.
  • Certification claims: If a listing claims FDA or LFGB compliance, check if it includes test report documentation in the product images.

FAQ

Are AliExpress air fryer accessories the same quality as Amazon ones?

Often, yes — or very close. Many AliExpress sellers supply the same factories that produce accessories for both markets. The key variable is the specific seller and their quality control. Look for high ratings (95%+), photo reviews, and listings that explicitly mention material specs (304 stainless steel, PFOA-free coating, etc.). Generic listings with no specs are a lower confidence buy.

How do I know if an AliExpress accessory will fit my air fryer?

Check the product listing's "compatible with" section and cross-reference with your air fryer's model number (usually on a label on the bottom of the unit). Common model numbers: Ninja FD401/AF100, Cosori CP158/CAF-P565S, Instant Pot Vortex Plus 6.25QT, Philips Premium Airfryer XXL. Most listings show a compatibility chart. When in doubt, measure your existing basket's diameter and depth and compare before ordering.

What's the typical delivery time from AliExpress?

Standard AliExpress shipping typically takes 15–25 days from China. Some sellers offer faster options (e.g., EU warehouse or expedited shipping) that can cut this to 7–12 days for a higher shipping fee. If you need a part urgently, factor this in — a replacement basket from AliExpress is better bought proactively than as an emergency.

Can I return items if they don't fit?

AliExpress has buyer protection that covers items not as described or significantly different from the listing. If an accessory doesn't fit your air fryer, you can open a dispute for a refund. Most established sellers also offer free returns. Always check the specific seller's return policy before purchasing.
